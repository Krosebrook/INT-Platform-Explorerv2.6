## 15-SECTION FRAMEWORK (Applied to Each Persona)

### Section 1: Demographics & Profile
- Age Range
- Years of Experience
- Education & Certifications
- Geographic Location
- Company Size Focus
- Industry Background
- Typical Title Variations
- Reporting Structure (for back of house)

### Section 2: Goals & Objectives
- 6-10 primary goals
- Short-term vs. long-term priorities
- Personal career goals vs. organizational goals
- Success definition from their perspective

### Section 3: Pain Points & Challenges
- 8-12 specific daily frustrations
- Systemic blockers and constraints
- Resource limitations
- Political/organizational challenges
- Market/competitive pressures

### Section 4: Motivations & Values
- Intrinsic motivators (what drives them personally)
- Extrinsic motivators (career, compensation, recognition)
- Core values and decision-making principles
- Risk tolerance and change appetite

### Section 5: Technology Proficiency
- Overall technical skill level (Novice/Medium/Expert)
- Specific platform proficiencies
- Learning style and adaptation speed
- Comfort with new technology adoption
- Preferred interfaces and tools

### Section 6: Decision Authority & Budget
- Spending authority levels
- Approval processes required
- Budget ranges (annual)
- Decision-making autonomy
- Influence on broader organizational decisions

### Section 7: Success Metrics & KPIs
- 6-12 measurable outcomes
- Individual performance metrics
- Team/department metrics
- Organizational impact metrics
- How success is evaluated (quarterly reviews, annual, etc.)

### Section 8: Typical Day/Week Breakdown
- Time allocation percentages
- Daily routines and rhythms
- Weekly recurring activities
- Quarterly/annual cyclical patterns
- Technology decision touchpoints

### Section 9: Communication Preferences
- Preferred formats (reports, presentations, dashboards, conversation)
- Channel preferences (email, phone, video, in-person)
- Frequency expectations (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly)
- Communication style (data-driven, creative, strategic, tactical)
- Meeting preferences

### Section 10: Information Sources & Influences
- Primary research sources (publications, analysts, conferences)
- Trusted advisors and peer networks
- Professional associations and communities
- Vendor/partner influence patterns
- Social media and online communities

### Section 11: Objections & Concerns
- 5-8 typical objections in buying/working process
- Underlying concerns and fears
- Risk evaluation criteria
- Trust-building requirements
- Red flags and deal-breakers

### Section 12: Buying/Working Process Journey
- Step-by-step decision journey (5-7 steps)
- Typical timeline (weeks or months)
- Key stakeholders involved at each stage
- Evaluation criteria and scoring
- Fast-track scenarios and triggers

### Section 13: INT Inc. Service Needs & Touchpoints
- Primary services (critical/frequently purchased)
- Secondary services (occasional/complementary)
- Engagement model preferences (project vs. retainer, phased vs. comprehensive)
- Key touchpoints across customer lifecycle (sales, onboarding, delivery, renewal)
- Success criteria for services

### Section 14: AI Tool Recommendations
- Primary platform recommendation (with specific rationale)
- Secondary platform options (use case specific)
- Implementation approach (phased, pilot, rollout)
- Expected ROI (time savings, cost avoidance, quality improvement)
- Security/compliance considerations
- Training requirements

### Section 15: Relationship Map
- Reports To (organizational hierarchy)
- Direct Reports (if manager/leader)
- Key Collaborators (cross-functional relationships)
- Influences (decision areas and scope)
- Influenced By (internal/external factors)
